What is Pisay

Pisay is the shortened term of Philippine Science High School (PSHS). It is a public, specialized high school system in the Philippines that operates as an attached agency of the Department of Science and Technology. It was established in 1963 by Republic Act 3661 to provide scholarships to high school students with exceptional aptitude in science and mathematics. The PSHS offers a curriculum that emphasizes science and mathematics and is designed to prepare students for careers in science and technology. PSHS has 16 campuses all over the Philippines.

How to become a Pisay scholar?

  1. Check for eligibility
  2. Watch out for the application period. You may check PSHS's Facebook pages.
  3. Apply online at nce.pshs.edu.ph or get a copy of the forms at any PSHS Campus, DOST office, or Provincial Science and Technology Center nearest you or download from www.pshs.nce.edu.ph
  4. Review for NCE (National Competitive Examination).
